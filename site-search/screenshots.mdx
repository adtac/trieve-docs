### Chat mode

The Trieve Chat mode offers a conversational AI chatbot that can answer questions and provide search results.
Additionally, it displays an option to provide rank the quality of an AI answer.

    <img src="https://cdn.trieve.ai/landing-tabs/dark-mode-aichat.webp" />

### Analytics

The component by default captures analytics on all searches and interactions with the search component.
This includes:

- Clicks
- Popular searches
- Low Confidence answers
